Workshop 1: “Emotions and Animals”

A space to discover how animals feel, how they communicate with us, and how we can build a relationship based on empathy.

The Emotional Experience

Connecting through feeling

“Emotions and Animals” is a workshop designed to humanize our gaze towards wildlife and pets.

Through interactive dynamics, we learn to identify joy, fear, or stress in animals, fostering a deeper and more respectful bond.

What will we explore?

Body language, sounds, and behaviors that tell us how they feel. We will learn to approach them safely and respectfully.

For whom?

Ideal for children and families who want to improve their coexistence with animals and develop their emotional intelligence.

Workshop Details

EMPATHY · COMMUNICATION · SENSITIVITY

What does the workshop include?

  • Interpretation game: “What is this animal telling me?”.
  • Mirror dynamic: exploring the emotions we share with them.
  • Practical guide: tips for a positive and stress-free approach.
  • Sensory workshop: creating toys or stimuli for their welfare.

“Animals speak to us with their gaze, their body, and their silence. We just need to learn to listen.”
