Campus 1: Living Nature

An immersive experience to connect with nature, learn about sustainability, and discover the life that surrounds us.

What is Living Nature?

“Living Nature” is a campus dedicated to coexistence with the natural environment. Participants will learn to observe, respect, and act for biodiversity through practical and reflective activities.

  • Exploration of local ecosystems.
  • Coexistence with animals from the shelter.
  • Sustainable and ecological actions.
  • Reflection on the relationship between humans and nature.

Experiential Methodology

OBSERVE · FEEL · ACT

The campus combines workshops, outings, and dynamics to foster environmental awareness and an emotional connection with nature.

Living Nature

Explore biodiversity

Guided walks and observation of flora and fauna to understand the interdependence of ecosystems.

Learn with animals

Coexistence activities with animals from the shelter to understand their emotions and behaviors.

Act for the planet

Recycling workshops, ecological actions, and collective projects to reduce our environmental footprint.

Campus Details

  • Duration: 3 intensive days.
  • Participants: youth and adults interested in sustainability.
  • Activities: workshops, outings, dynamics, and guided reflections.
  • Goal: to foster ecological awareness and respect for the environment.

Join Living Nature

If you want to be part of this transformative experience, sign up for the campus and experience nature from within. Every step is an opportunity to learn and care for the planet.
