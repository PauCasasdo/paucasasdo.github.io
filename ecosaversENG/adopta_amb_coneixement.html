Adopt with Knowledge

Workshop Photography

Why this workshop?

Adopting an animal is an important decision that involves responsibility, commitment, and knowledge. In this workshop, we explore what it truly means to live with an animal and how to do it in a respectful way.

What will we learn?

  • Real expectations about living with animals.
  • Animal welfare and basic needs.
  • Family responsibility and distribution of tasks.
  • Adoption vs. buying: what each one entails.
  • Empathy and respect towards animals.

Who is it for?

Ideal for families, schools, and groups who want to better understand what it means to adopt an animal and how to do so responsibly.

Main Goal

To provide tools so that adoption decisions are informed, responsible, and beneficial for both people and animals.

“An animal is not an object: it is a life companion that deserves respect and understanding.”
