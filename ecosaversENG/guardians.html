Activity “Guardians of the Habitat”

An environmental education activity to discover the importance of ecosystems, the threats they face, and how we can become their protectors.

The Guardians Experience

Protecting life

“Guardians of the Habitat” is a proposal aimed at raising awareness about the vulnerability of natural environments and the fauna that inhabits them.

Through observation and play, participants learn to value biodiversity and understand their role in its conservation.

What will we learn?

We will discover the key species of our environment, how they live, and what happens when their habitat is altered or destroyed.

Our mission

To foster a proactive attitude towards the environment. To pass from being spectators to becoming active guardians of nature.

Activity Details

BIODIVERSITY · ACTION · FUTURE

What does the activity include?

  • Ecosystem exploration: identifying local fauna and flora.
  • Environmental challenges: interactive games about sustainability.
  • Observation workshop: using field tools like binoculars and guides.
  • Conservation talk: learning about real protection projects.
  • Guardian diploma: recognition for all participants.

“When we know nature, we learn to love it. When we love it, we protect it.”
