A campus to take action: learn, create, and transform our environment with sustainable and collaborative initiatives.

Active Planet

What is Active Planet?

“Active Planet” is a campus that promotes active participation in sustainability and ecological innovation projects. Participants will learn to turn ideas into real actions to care for the planet.

  • Creation of collaborative ecological projects.
  • Practical learning about energy and recycling.
  • Environmental volunteering activities.
  • Reflection on the impact of our actions.

Active Methodology

LEARN · DO · TRANSFORM

The campus combines theory and practice with workshops, ecological challenges, and group dynamics to foster creativity and environmental responsibility.

Innovate with green ideas

Develop sustainable projects that improve your environment and reduce environmental impact.

Collaborate with your team

Work in groups to design real ecological actions and share knowledge.

Transform your surroundings

Apply what you learn to generate positive and lasting changes in your daily life.

Campus Details

  • Duration: 2 intensive days.
  • Participants: youth and adults with an active and creative spirit.
  • Activities: workshops, ecological challenges, collaborative projects.
  • Goal: to foster innovation and sustainable action.

Join Active Planet

If you want to be part of a movement that transforms ideas into actions, sign up for the campus and help build a more active and sustainable planet.
