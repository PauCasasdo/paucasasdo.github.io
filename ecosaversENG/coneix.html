Get to know the Shelter

An immersive visit to discover how the shelter works, meet the animals that live here, and learn about the importance of their care and welfare.

The Shelter Experience

Inside the shelter

The activity “Get to know the Shelter” is an opportunity to see first-hand the daily work carried out to protect and care for abandoned animals.

Ideal for families, schools, and groups who want to learn about animal protection from an educational and respectful perspective.

What will we do?

We will take a guided tour of the facilities, explain the residents' stories, and learn about the basic needs of the animals.

Why visit us?

Because knowing their reality is the first step toward respecting them. It is an educational experience that builds empathy and awareness.

Activity Details

AWARENESS · EDUCATION · RESPECT

What does the visit include?

  • Guided tour: access to the different areas of the shelter.
  • Meet the residents: learn about the animals' stories and characters.
  • Education session: talk about animal welfare and responsible ownership.
  • Interaction: safe and supervised contact with some animals.
  • Questions and answers: space to clear doubts with our team.

“When you get to know the shelter, you understand that every gesture can change an animal's life.”
