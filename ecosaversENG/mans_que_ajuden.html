Volunteering Day “Helping Hands”

A day dedicated to active collaboration in the shelter: maintenance, care, and improvement of the animals' living spaces.

The Volunteering Experience

Together for them

“Helping Hands” is a day designed for those who want to get involved practically in the daily life of the shelter.

Your time and your hands can make a difference in the quality of life of the animals that are waiting for a home.

What will we do?

From cleaning and repairing facilities to preparing food or improving the environmental enrichment of the animal areas.

Who can participate?

Anyone with a desire to help! No prior experience is needed, just commitment and respect for the animals.

Day Details

COLLABORATION · COMMITMENT · WELFARE

What does the day include?

  • Initial briefing: organization of tasks and safety rules.
  • Hands-on work: collaboration in various maintenance tasks.
  • Social break: space to share experiences with other volunteers.
  • Shelter talk: information on how to continue collaborating with ECOSAVERS.

“When we help animals, we also help ourselves.”
