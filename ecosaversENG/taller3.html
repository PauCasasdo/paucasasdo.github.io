Workshop 3: “Green Footprints”

Learn to reduce your environmental impact and protect biodiversity through recycling, upcycling, and sustainable habits.

The Sustainable Experience

Walking towards the future

“Green Footprints” is a practical workshop where we transform waste into resources and discover how our daily consumption affects the planet.

Sustainability is not just a word; it is a series of small actions that together create a huge global change.

What will we learn?

Creative recycling techniques, how to create an urban garden, and the importance of the circular economy for natural habitats.

Our Goal

To empower participants to become conscious consumers and active defenders of the environment.

Workshop Details

SUSTAINABILITY · CREATIVITY · NATURE

What does the workshop include?

  • Upcycling lab: creating useful objects from recycled materials.
  • Planting workshop: making seed bombs or small urban planters.
  • Eco-Challenge: a game to learn how to sort waste correctly.
  • Biodiversity talk: how recycling saves animal lives.

“Every green step we take leaves a positive footprint on the planet.”
