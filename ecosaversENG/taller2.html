Workshop 2: “Shelter in Action”

Discover the daily life of an animal shelter: from basic care to the management and cleaning of common spaces.

The Practical Experience

Learning by doing

“Shelter in Action” is an immersive experience where participants take an active role in animal care.

An opportunity to understand the work behind a shelter and the responsibility required to keep animals healthy and happy.

What will we do?

Feeding tasks, grooming, area maintenance, and an introduction to the rescue and adoption protocol.

Key Value

Fostering responsibility and commitment. Understanding that animal welfare depends on constant and dedicated work.

Workshop Details

RESPONSIBILITY · CARE · DEDICATION

What does the workshop include?

  • Nutrition workshop: preparing specific diets for each species.
  • Space management: cleaning and organizing animal habitats.
  • Health and Hygiene: basic notions of grooming and veterinary care.
  • Interactive talk: "The journey of a rescued animal".

“Every action in the shelter is an opportunity to give life, hope, and respect.”
