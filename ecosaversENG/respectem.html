Activity “Respecting Animals”

An educational experience designed to foster empathy, compassion, and basic knowledge about animal welfare from an early age.

The Respect Experience

Empathy as a foundation

“Respecting Animals” is more than a talk; it is an interactive workshop where we explore the emotions and needs of the living beings that share the planet with us.

Ideal for primary schools and families who want to educate in values of non-violence and universal respect.

What do we work on?

We identify animal body language, their basic needs, and how our actions directly impact their happiness and health.

Why choose it?

Fostering respect for animals is the first step toward creating a more balanced and supportive society for everyone.

Activity Details

EMPATHY · COEXISTENCE · VALUES

What does the activity include?

  • Dynamic workshop: identifying emotions through games.
  • Educational storytelling: stories that promote animal welfare.
  • Manual activity: creating awareness material to take home.
  • Safe interaction: guided experience with our socialized animals.

“Respecting animals is also a way of learning to respect each other.”
