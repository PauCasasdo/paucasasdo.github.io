Charity Route “Walking with Paws”

A charity walk to enjoy nature, build community, and support the animals at the shelter. A day to move, share, and help.

Walking with Paws

A day to walk and help

The Charity Route “Walking with Paws” is an activity open to everyone: families, children, youth, volunteers, and animal lovers. An accessible walk full of good vibes.

Participating is a direct way to support the shelter and the animals that live there.

What will we do?

We will walk through a natural route suitable for all ages, with informative stops, activities, and moments to meet animals from the shelter.

Why is it charitable?

All proceeds go entirely to animal welfare: food, veterinary care, and improvements to the shelter spaces.

Route Details

SPORT · NATURE · SOLIDARITY

What does the day include?

  • Guided route: nature trail with monitors.
  • Refreshment point: water and fruit for participants.
  • Family activities: games and challenges during the route.
  • Animal area: get to know some of the shelter residents.
  • Final photo: group photography to remember the day.

“When we walk together, we move toward a better future for animals.”
